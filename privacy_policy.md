# Privacy Policy for Ono Master

**Last updated: March 25, 2026**

Ono Master ("we", "our", or "us") provides this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection
- **Account Information:** If you sign in with Google, we may collect your display name and email address provided by Google.
- **Gameplay Data:** We collect game-related data (wins, losses, points) to manage leadboards and game features.
- **Device Information:** We may collect basic device info to ensure app stability.

## 2. Use of Data
The data we collect is used solely to:
- Provide and improve game features.
- Maintain global leaderboards.
- Display relevant advertisements (via AdMob).
- Offer in-app purchases (via Google Play Billing).

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase:** For real-time multiplayer coordination and data storage.
- **Google AdMob:** To display advertisements.
- **Google Play Services:** For authentication and ranking.

## 4. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time within the app settings or by contacting us.
